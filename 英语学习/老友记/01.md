## 句子 1：There's nothing to tell.
> 没什么好说的，他只不过是我的同事

### 句型
`There's nothing to + V.`

### 造句练习

1. ✅ There's nothing to worry about.

2. ❌ ~~There's nothing to look.~~
   - 正确：There's nothing to **look at**.
   - 原因：`look` 是不及物动词，需要介词 `at` 才能接宾语
   - 类似：There's nothing to laugh at. / There's nothing to talk about.

3. ✅ There's nothing to talk about.

4. ✅ There's nothing to eat.

## You're going out with a guy.
> 和你交往的男人...

### 句型
go out with + 人

表示和某人约会、交往（谈恋爱那种），不是泛指「一起出门玩」时必须用这个意思；也可以说 see someone 表示在跟谁约会。

结构：be + going out with + 人

### 造句练习

1. You're going out with your boss.
2. I'm going out with a girl.
3. She's going out with someone from class.
4. He's going out with a girl.
5. He wouldn't go out with you just for fun.
6. Come on. We all know you're going out with Jake.
别装了，我们都知道你在跟 Jake 约会。
7. Come on. You said you weren't going out with anyone.
你之前还说没在跟谁交往呢。

## 句子 4：There's gotta be something wrong with him.
> 多多少少一定有点问题
> `gotta` = have got to，表示"必须/一定"

### 句型
`There's gotta be + something + wrong with + 人/物`

口语里用 `There's gotta be...` 表示推断：「肯定/一定（有某种情况）」。更正式可写 `There has got to be...` 或 `There must be...`。

- `something wrong with + 人/物` = 某人/某物「有问题、不对劲」
- 同类：`nothing wrong with...`（没什么问题）、`What's wrong with...?`（怎么了？）

### 造句练习

1. ✅ There's gotta be something wrong with this Wi‑Fi.
   这 Wi‑Fi 肯定有问题。

2. ✅ There's gotta be something wrong with the schedule — none of us got the memo.
   日程肯定搞错了——我们谁都没收到通知。

3. ❌ ~~There's gotta be something wrong **on** him.~~
   - 正确：There's gotta be something wrong **with** him.
   - 原因：表示「某人有问题」用介词 `with`，不用 `on`。

4. ✅ There's gotta be a reason she left early.
   她提早走一定有原因。

5. ✅ If the test says negative but you still feel sick, there's gotta be something else going on.
   如果检测是阴性但你还是难受，那一定是还有别的情况。

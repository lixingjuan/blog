## 句子 1：There's nothing to tell.
> 没什么好说的，他只不过是我的同事

### 句型
`There's nothing to + V.`

### 造句练习

1. ✅ There's nothing to worry about.

2. ❌ ~~There's nothing to look.~~
   - 正确：There's nothing to **look at**.
   - 原因：`look` 是不及物动词，需要介词 `at` 才能接宾语
   - 类似：There's nothing to laugh at. / There's nothing to talk about.

3. ✅ There's nothing to talk about.

4. ✅ There's nothing to eat.

## 句子 2：It's just some guy I work with.

### 句型

3. It's just a scratch.
4. It's just a misunderstanding.
5. It's just a joke.
